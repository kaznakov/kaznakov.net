ScanYOmail — Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information when you scan and organize your physical mail.

Effective: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ScanYOmail ("we", "us", or "the app") processes information when you use the app. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

1. Data controller

ScanYOmail is developed and operated by the developer listed in the Contact section below. Depending on how the app is configured, your data may be processed:

  • On-device (locally on your phone/tablet), and/or
  • On a private backend server used to provide core functionality (for example, OCR and extraction).

2. Information we process

2.1 Scanned letters

  • Images (photos) of letters you scan using your device camera.
  • Basic technical information about the image (file size, format).

2.2 Mail metadata (created by you or extracted)

  • Letter title and type (for example: bill, banking, government, promo, other).
  • Sender name or description (for example: "Utility company").
  • Scan date and time.
  • Optional extracted fields such as amount due, due date, account/reference numbers (if present on the letter).

2.3 Events and reminders

  • Payment and deadline events linked to your letters.
  • Event status (pending, overdue, done).
  • Optional local notifications scheduled on your device (if enabled).

2.4 Diagnostics (limited)

  • Basic app diagnostics required to operate the service (for example, request timestamps and errors).
We do not sell your data. We do not show third-party ads in ScanYOmail and we do not build advertising profiles from your content.

3. How we use your data

We use the information described above only to provide the app’s core features, such as:

  • Displaying your scanned letters in the Mailbox.
  • Extracting and storing key details (for example, amounts and dates) to help you track bills and deadlines.
  • Creating and updating events and reminders.
  • Showing upcoming payments and deadlines on the Home and Calendar screens.

4. Sharing

We share your information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers (if enabled): If OCR/AI processing is enabled, images or extracted text may be sent to third-party processors (for example, OCR/AI APIs) over encrypted connections to return recognized text or structured data.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. OCR and AI processing

ScanYOmail may use OCR (text recognition) and AI models to extract information from your scanned letters. This is used to identify useful details such as due dates, amounts, and sender names.

  • When cloud processing is used, only the data needed for recognition/extraction is transmitted.
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted connections.
  • Providers process the content to return recognized text or structured results.
Important: AI extraction may be imperfect. You should verify critical information (amounts, due dates) against the original letter.

6. Storage and retention

  • Scanned letters, metadata, and events are stored either locally on your device and/or on a private backend used by the app.
  • We keep data for as long as needed to provide the app’s functionality or until you delete it from inside the app.
  • When you delete a letter, the app also removes its associated events and the link to the stored image (subject to technical constraints and backups).

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect stored data from accidental loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your choices and controls

Inside the app you can:

  • Delete individual letters and their associated events.
  • Adjust settings for automatic event creation and reminders (if available).
  • Revoke camera permission at any time in your device Settings.
  • Stop using the app and uninstall it from your device.

9. Children’s data

ScanYOmail is designed for adult users who manage their own mail and payments. It is not intended for children under 16 years of age.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as ScanYOmail evolves. When important changes are made, we will update the policy on this page and may also update the text inside the app.
